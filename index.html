OpenSanctions

OpenSanctions is a global database of persons and companies of political, criminal, or economic interest. It combines sanctions lists, databases of politically exposed persons, and other public information into a single, easy-to-access dataset.

  • Cross-check leaks and public databases for possible conflicts of interests and signs of illicit activity.
  • Track political conflicts and compare world-wide sanctions policies.
  • Check potential customers and partners in international dealings.
